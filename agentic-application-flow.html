Member of Technical Staff,
Agentic Banking

Remote — Full-Time United States / Portugal Engineering · Banking Solutions Applies via agent session Builder Grant · identity-gated

Anchorage Digital is building the rails autonomous agents bank on. As a member of the Agentic Banking team, you will design the identity, allowance, and audit infrastructure that lets an AI agent hold and move money without anyone losing sleep. Our Village culture means a laptop can be shipped, but our culture cannot.

ATechnical Skills

  • Deep experience with distributed systems where correctness is non-negotiable: payments, custody, settlement, or comparable regulated infrastructure.
  • Hands-on work orchestrating LLM-driven agents in production: tool use, policy enforcement, structured output, evaluation harnesses.
  • Fluency in Go, TypeScript, or Rust, and comfort reading model behavior the way you read a stack trace.

BComplexity and Impact of Work

  • Design controls that make autonomy scale safely: Know Your Agent identity, allowance-scoped accounts, spending policy, and immutable audit trails.
  • Decide where an agent's authority ends and a human's accountability begins, then encode that boundary in systems regulators can examine.

COrganizational Knowledge

  • Work across custody, compliance, and banking solutions to ensure agentic products inherit the same standard as everything else the bank ships.

DCommunication and Influence

  • Write with precision. On this team your specs, your reviews, and your prompts are read by both humans and machines, and both act on them.

EYou may be a fit for this role if you

  • Have shipped a system where a model held real responsibility, and you can describe exactly how it failed and what you changed.
  • Think in failure modes first and treat constraints as the thing that makes autonomy possible, not the thing that slows it down.

FAlthough not a requirement, bonus points if you

  • Have given an agent a budget and watched, carefully, what it did with it.
  • Have published prompt-engineering, evaluation, or agent-safety work in any form.

GCompensation at Anchorage Digital

Market-leading salary and equity, alongside a full benefits package. Compensation is determined by role, level, and location.

A different kind of application

Apply by directing an agent

This role reviews how you direct machines, not just what you have shipped. Instead of a take-home, your application includes a 45-minute agent workbench session: you direct a live agent through a scoped task on a sandboxed allowance.

The session agent carries a verifiable identity and a scoped allowance before it can act. We bank agents this way, and we hire their operators the same way.
Your prompts are the assessment. We analyze how you decompose, constrain, verify, and recover.
Every prompt is logged to an audit trail and reviewed by the team alongside your application. No hidden scoring.
This is one door into the Anchorage Builder Grant: any identity-verified builder can claim the same $20 allowance and build on Anchorage rails. Standout builds fast-track here. Outputs are retained on Anchorage rails under the grant license.
