Anchorage Builder Grant · Concept

Hire the people who can direct machines — on the rails built for machines.

An identity-gated $20 builder allowance that doubles as the world's first agentic hiring funnel.

Prepared by Winn · June 2026
The problem

Agentic engineers are the scarcest hire in fintech.

A

The fee is enormous ~20% of OTE

Recruiters charge ~20% of OTE for senior/agentic engineering placements — at $250K–$500K OTE, that's $50K–$100K per hire.

B

The instruments miss the skill

Résumés and LeetCode don't measure the actual skill: directing autonomous agents safely.

decomposition constraints verification recovery
C

Everyone bids on the same paper

Every other company is bidding for the same résumés — nobody is measuring the actual work.

The mechanism

Four stages. One funnel.

01

Identity

LinkedIn OAuth sign-in + facial liveness. Full KYC gates any real-value tier.

02

Allowance

A KYA-signed session agent: $20 sandboxed, hard cap, scoped tools — billed against the OAuth identity.

03

Workbench

The candidate directs a live agent. Every prompt is logged and scored on four dimensions:

decompositionconstraintsverificationrecovery

Retention

Session replay + artifacts retained on Anchorage rails under grant license.

How we score

We grade judgment — and gate on the one thing a bank can't get wrong.

Decomposition Staged the work and defined "done" before invoking the agent. process
Constraints Set ceilings, scoped tools, and refused an injected instruction in untrusted tool output. curveball
Verification Demanded independent proof — live call, receipt, ledger reconciled to the cent. process
Recovery Reversal first on failure; rebuilt selection criteria from what broke. process
◆ The cardinal-failure gate

One catastrophic instinct outweighs ten clever turns.

Approving the injected transfer, settling on unverified KYC, or blind-retrying a double-charge caps the score regardless of brilliance elsewhere. ≥60% of the score comes from deterministic signals in the audit log — not a single judge's opinion.

What we uniquely measure

The gap no existing assessment fills.

  • Budgeted real-credit direction — every benchmark optimizes accuracy and ignores cost.
  • Compliance discipline under pressure — does the operator catch the agent shortcutting a KYC step?
  • Recovery from silent tool failure in a live payment flow.
  • Rollback & partial-state remediation mid-settlement.
  • Conservative escalation — halting when risk exceeds threshold vs. a confident wrong action.
Checked: SWE-bench · τ-bench · AgentDojo · Mercor APEX · HackerRank — none stage these. A non-bank can't.
